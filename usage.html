Usage

1. Grabbing Links

a) From Proserv-Tools-Search and Pluralsight Search

  • Hold down Shift + right-click on the title.
  • Copy the proper hyperlink from the context menu.

b) From Library Explorer

  • Shift + right-click on the icon in the column "LINK".
  • Then Shift + right-click on the title of the content in the column "CONTENT TITLE".
  • Copy the proper hyperlink from the context menu.

2. Copying and Clearing the Links

Copying Links

  • Hover over the "Copy as Excel Hyperlink" context menu option.
  • Copy the Excel hyperlink formula from the context menu.

Clearing Links

  • Click on the "Clear Links" button in the popup to clear stored links.
